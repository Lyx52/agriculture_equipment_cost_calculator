Row-Crop tractor
Built in Geelong, Australia
The McCormick International FARMALL A-514 was mechanically similar to the British-built B-450 and similar in appearance to the US-built 560. A row-crop tractor, it was available with single front wheel, tricycle front, or adjustable wide front. The A-514 was supplanted by the A-554.
